## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: "401 Unauthorized" when logging in
**Cause:** Wrong email format or typo in email  
**Solution:** Check the exact email from the tables above. Note that doctor emails follow the format: `dr.firstname.lastname@chennaihospital.com`

### Issue: "No patients found" on Patients page
**Cause:** Was filtering by location but patients have no location assigned  
**Solution:** Fixed - patients are now shown without location filter

### Issue: Menu items redirect to landing page
**Cause:** Broken routes in navigation  
**Solution:** Fixed - see ROUTING_ISSUES_AUDIT.md for details
